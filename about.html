Our History & Narrative

The Story of KGN Classes

KGN Classes was established in 2012 in the heart of Govandi West, Mumbai, with a singular mission: to provide high-quality, comprehensive, and affordable coaching to students who need it most. Our founder, Prof. Wali Khan, recognized that many bright minds are held back due to lack of customized attention and high educational fees. KGN Classes was formed to break those barriers.

We specialize in tutoring secondary school and junior college sections, covering all major streams—Science, Commerce, and Arts. By maintaining manageable batch sizes, we make sure that our teachers can connect directly with each student, diagnosing their learning strengths and building their academic confidence step by step.

Director's Message

A Vision of Grassroots Mentorship

"Educational institutions are not commercial enterprises; they are standard-bearers of future generations. At KGN Classes, we ensure that no student gets lost in the crowd. Our teaching methods are designed around individual progress."

Prof. Wali Khan’s philosophy centers around patience, rigorous practice, and accessibility. He believes that coaching should extend beyond typical lecture hours. Faculty members at KGN Classes are trained to be accessible around the clock, providing a supportive safety net for students tackling difficult syllabus sheets or board exams.

We prepare our students to master concepts deeply rather than simply memorizing them. This approach has led to outstanding academic results, with KGN Classes students constantly securing stellar scores across SSC and HSC Board examinations in Mumbai.

Our Journey

Milestones & Timeline

Look back at how KGN Classes has grown since its inception over a decade ago.

2012

The Foundation

KGN Classes opens its doors in Govandi West. Founded by Prof. Wali Khan, we began with a single classroom and a small batch of passionate school students.

2015

Stream Expansion

Driven by parent requests, we introduced dedicated junior college batch programs, launching structured curricula for Science and Commerce streams.

2019

Arts Stream & Mock Tests

To support humanities students, KGN Classes launched its Arts curriculum. We also incorporated computerized tracking of unit tests to monitor student performance.

2023

Record-Breaking Scores

Our students scored historic results in both SSC (10th) and HSC (12th) boards, establishing KGN Classes as a high-success hub in local Mumbai divisions.

2026

Over 1500 Stories

Entering our 14th year of service, we celebrate thousands of successful alumni who graduated to prominent professional fields across engineering, finance, and arts.
